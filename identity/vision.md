# 130AfterM — Project Vision

130AfterM is my personal corner of the internet.

It is **not** a portfolio.
It is **not** a trading signal website.
It is **not** a course funnel.
It is **not** an influencer brand.

Think of it as a modern evolution of a MySpace profile. If MySpace had continued evolving into 2026 instead of disappearing, this is what my personal page would feel like.

The website exists to document who I am, what I'm interested in, and what I'm building—not to sell anything.

It is an operating system for my identity.
